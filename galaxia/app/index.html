Upcoming features

Here are some of the mechanics and features we’re adding to the Galaxia universe.

  • MISSION LAUNCH

    Prepare the Gen 1 planets and corporations
    ready for launch.

  • GEN 2 PLANETS + GALAXIA WIKI

    Add extra moons and planets to the universe
    as well as a dedicated wiki.

  • GALACTIC MAP

    Create an interactive, explorable
    map of the Galaxia universe.

  • EXPLORE. UPGRADE. MINE.

    Scour the universe for new planets and
    profit from the ones you already have.

  • MOON CREDITS

    Enable planets to passively mine moon credits,
    which are collected by corporations.

  • CORP MANAGEMENT

    Let users upgrade planet infrastructure,
    send expeditions and manage corp finances.
