Role description

As a Front-End developer within the Comviq Join squad you will be responsible for building great customer experiences. The squad support new customers from when they show purchase interest until their service is in use.

The squad ensure that customers have a good experience all the way from when they looking to buy one of our products and commit to a purchase, to when they receive their product and start using it. You will work in a dynamic and forward-thinking environment.

As a Front-end developer within D&C you are also a part of a chapter, composed of nine Front-end developers, meeting on a weekly basis to share knowledge and exchange thoughts and ideas. In this role you will collaborate closely with other teams when needed. 80% of your role will focus on development and 20% with demo and retro refinement.
