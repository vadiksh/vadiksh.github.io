Jose Mourinho accepts 12 month jail sentence

  • 7 hours ago
  • Tom Kennedy

Former Manchester United manager Jose Mourinho has been served with a 12 months suspended jail sentence.

The 56-year-old is accused of committing tax fraud between 2011 and 2012 when he coached Spanish giants Real Madrid. The Portuguese manager will however not spend any time in prison as the sentence is for less than two years.

In 2016, Mourinho’s representatives Gestifute released a statement insisting he was “fully compliant with tax obligations” after allegations of tax evasion first came to light via the whistleblowing platform ‘Football Leaks’’
