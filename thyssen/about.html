Thyssen Trading

Thyssen Trading is a specialized independent representative of reputed importers and exporters of conventional and organic spices, seeds & herbs. We are mainly active in Europe, Asia, The Middle East, Africa and North America. In more than half a decade we have gained a firm reputation for adding value to both our importers and exporters operations. Our expert service is personal and with an exacting attention to detail in order to cater every aspect of your needs. The importers and exporters we work with are carefully selected and audited in order to assure a mutually beneficial cooperation. Our partners choose to work with Thyssen Trading because we are experienced, reliable, accessible, flexible and always most competitive in price and delivery terms.
