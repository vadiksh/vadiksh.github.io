General conditions

These general terms and conditions apply to all log cabin room bookings only.

  • All rates shown are in Canadian Dollars. We accept credit card (Visa/Mastercard), cheque, or wire transfer. To guarantee the booking, we require a credit card on file or at booking, a deposit of 50% of the accommodation costs.
  • Bookings of 3 log cabin rooms or more are considered group bookings and require full payment for the total amount of the reservation 30 days prior of arrival.
  • Minimum stay of two nights in High Season in Log Cabin Rooms.
  • Minimum stay of two nights in Mid Season, one night on request in Log Cabin Rooms.
  • Continental breakfast buffet included.
  • All room rates are subject to 15 % tax (GST, hotel and tourism tax), for camping 5% tax applies.
  • All prices based on Double Occupancy.
  • Every extra person is $40.00 per night - Children up to 4th birthday are free of charge - Children from 4th to 12th birthday are $28.00 per night
  • Single night surcharge $30.00 per night.
  • 10% reduction available for long-term stays of 5 days and more.

Ranch rules

In order to maintain Wells Gray Guest Ranch safe, wholesome family atmosphere, each guest using the property will be expected to respect the following:

  • - No pets are allowed at the Ranch.
  • - Smoking of tobacco is permitted only in designated areas
    Smoking and vaping of cannabis is prohibited at the Ranch.
  • - Campfires must be contained in existing, designated fire-pits.
  • - No fireworks or explosive devices of any kind are permitted.

    Campground

    Tenting

    The Ranch has 3 well maintained, grassed campsites, each with roomy picnic shelters and fire pits for tenting groups mainly. There’s an amenity block with hot showers, toilets, dishwashing area and a fresh drinking water station within short walking distance. Reservation is required. All prices for double occupancy and subject to 5% GST

    RV's

    We currently have only a few spots for RV’s available. No electric or water hook up! There’s an amenity block with hot showers, toilets, dishwashing area and a fresh drinking water station within short walking distance. Please contact us for further information. All prices for double occupancy and subject to 5% GST

    Bunkhouses

    The 6 bunkhouses provide simple accommodation and are set back in a remote area of the ranch with two outhouses nearby. The shower-house with toilets and dishwashing area is in about 3 minutes walking distance. These little cabins are like the old ranch-hand accommodation very basic with no electricity and water. They feature 2 – 10 wooden bunkbeds with covered mattresses. Bring your own pillow, sleeping bag and a good flashlight. Our bunkhouses are only open to groups of a minimum of 10 guests. Please contact us for rates and bookings.

    Canoeing & Sightseeing

    Wells Gray Appetizer

    The best way of exploring Wells Gray Park is walking short nature- and historic trails along the Wells Gray Park corridor combined with paddling the crystal clear waters of Clearwater Lake.

    This trip starts with meeting at 9.30 am at the ranch desk. Leave your car at the ranch and join us in our suitable vehicle to travel the dirt roads. During this trip a knowledgeable local guide will drive you and show you the major sights of Wells Gray Park.

    The highlights of the day is an approx. 2 hour canoe trip on Clearwater Lake to Caribou Campground, where you enjoy the fresh made lunch.

    Beginners welcome as our licensed and trained guides will give you detailed paddling and safety instructions.

    Duration: approx. 7 hours

    Horseback Riding

    Riding

    Horseback riding is the featured activity at Wells Gray Ranch. All horseback riding is guided. Trail rides are anywhere from an hour to almost all day with lunch on the trail. We provide quality, seasoned horses to fit the needs of the first time rider as well as those more experienced.
    We enjoy taking people on a ride. If you have never ridden before, it’s not too late to try!
    All riding is by appointment only!

    Restrictions for trail riding

    Minimum age: 10 years old – exceptions may be made based on previous riding experience and size of the child. For children under 10 years old, lead-line on horseback is available.
    Maximum weight: 220 lbs or 105 kg. All riders must be physically fit and mentally capable of horseback riding. We reserve the right to deny riding to anyone.
