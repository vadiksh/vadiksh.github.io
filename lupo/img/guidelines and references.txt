Guidelines
- Should be responsive and adapt to mobile devices
- When mouse passes over the images they should enlighten in orange, see web sample.jpg, there's one of the images enlightened
- When mouse passes over the buttons from the top they should be replaced with hover and clicked versions when clicked.
- When mouse passes over a text link, such as the sample videogame review quotes, it should change to a brigther orange color.
- Screenshots should show in HD when clicked like in here: http://altosodyssey.com/
- The space background should move slower than the rest of the site to create a parallax effect
- Include widgets: Bandcamp, humble bundle
- Include mailing list at the foot
