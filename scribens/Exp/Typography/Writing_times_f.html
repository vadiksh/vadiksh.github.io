Writing times

 

à Writing the time out in a text has quite a variety of possibilities these days.  First of all, AM and PM have a range of acceptable formats, such as: A.M., P.M., am, pm, a.m., and p.m.  It is your choice whether you wish to separate the AM or PM from the time of day with a space or not.  Furthermore, some choose to write just the hour (e.g. 9pm), while others include the semicolon and minutes (e.g. 9:00pm).  Remember to stay consistent within a single text!

 

All of the following examples are accepted ways to write the time:

 

10am

 

10 A.M.

 

3 pm

 

3:15P.M.
