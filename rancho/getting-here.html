How to get here

After a 5 hour drive north from Vancouver, BC, or a 3.5 hour drive from Jasper, Alberta, turn off Hwy 5 (roundabout) in Clearwater to the Clearwater Valley Road, leading to Wells Gray Park.

Surrounded by parklands our property is located 27 km up this road within the park, 14 km past the park entrance , on the left hand side and clearly marked with signs.

Local attractions

Need a good excuse to get out of town? We’ll give you several!

Whether you choose to hike on the Trophy Mountain meadows, discover your inner cowboy/cowgirl on a horseback ride or paddle like the pioneers on the Murtle Lake, Wells Gray Park will put you in touch with the best nature has to offer.

The famous Wells Gray Park is only a short drive from our Ranch, 35 km from Clearwater and is an outdoor adventure central. This huge provincial park boasts 39 named waterfalls, including spectacular Helmcken Falls, one of the highest waterfalls in Canada!

Choose your adventure, serene or extreme – or a bit of both. Visitors come for camping, hiking, white-water rafting, horseback riding, golfing, fishing, tandem paragliding, scenic flights, wildlife viewing, boat tours and canoeing, just to name a few. Did you know that Wells Gray Park is simply the best place for canoe touring worldwide? Murtle Lake is the largest paddle-only lake in the world!
