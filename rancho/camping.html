Cabins & Camping

Explore Our log cabin rooms

The private log cabin rooms

Where chic meets nature, our handcrafted log cabins rooms are the definition of comfort and relaxation. The Ranch has 4 Deluxe Log Cabin Rooms and 6 Standard Log Cabin Rooms available for you.

The rooms are arranged side by side with separate entrances a front porch with view of the horse pasture and forest covered hills to kick back on after a long day on the trail.

    All cabins are equipped with full amenities:

  • Comfortable beds
  • Sitting area
  • Kitchen amenities: small fridge, coffee/tea bar and microwave and basic kitchen utensils
  • Private bathroom with toilet and shower. Hair dryer available

Explore Our camping area

Get outside

Say hello to the great outdoors! Camping is always an amazing experience to have and the best way to unplug and connect to nature. Planning on camping? Whether you’re staying in a tent, RV, rustic cabin or bunkhouse, you’ll find a wide range of on-site amenities that bring an added level of convenience to your stay, like roomy BBQ shelters, picnic tables, fire pits, firewood and clean washrooms/showers.

  • Tenting

    Tent camping is by far the most in touch with nature you will get. It is the true meaning of roughing it. The Ranch offers two nice lawn areas for camping. There’s an amenity block with hot showers toilets.

  • Bunkhouses

    The bunkhouses are currently only available for groups. These bunkhouses are wooden buildings with 2 to 10 bunk beds in each one, there are 6 bunkhouses.

  • Rustic camping cabins

    We know that for many adventurers, the way you stay has to inspire you and should be fun and easy. Keep the sense of adventure, the feeling of being closer to nature but add a few more home comforts. That’s why we’re offering our unique overnight options, the rustic camping cabins.
