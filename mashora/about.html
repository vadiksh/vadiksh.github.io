About us

Mashora Group is a boutique consultancy that provides services to government, institutional and private clients who pursue projects in the Middle East and Africa. We provide our clients with the most qualified expertise in each of our service areas. Our consultants are not just experts in various fields, they have on-ground experience from different contexts which enables them to have deep insights and analysis.
We provide services that are based on our candid advice and reliant on solid data. Our work is conducted in full transparency.

Our Objectives

  • Help governments, the private sector and international institutions analyze public policy challenges combining international comparative experience with in-depth local insights.

  • Assisting in the implementation of the proposed solutions and strategies.
