Our Services

We specialize in providing our consultation services and implementing projects in the following areas:

  • Access to Emerging
    Markets

    The Middle East and Africa is home to some of the most thriving emerging markets and business opportunities. With higher than average returns for investors, accelerated growth, and increasing demand patterns, emerging markets are the future. Unfortunately, many investors do not have the tools to access such new territory. This where we come to the picture. We provide both investors and local businesses in these emerging markets with the strategies for win-win scenarios.

  • Public Sector Reform

    This is one of the most common priorities for our government clients. It also one of the key areas for stability and development. Fragile countries are often crippled by overlybureaucratic and inefficient government institutions. Challenges ranging from fiscal, managerial and decentralization reforms are a priority for many governments in the age of globalization. We tailor to our clients country specific solutions and assist in their implementation.

  • Peacebuilding

    Peacebuilding is a multilayered process that encompasses different segments of society and strategic approaches. Whether government or non-governmental, our clients will be linked to some of the most experienced minds behind designing peacebuilding processes. Establishing peaceful foundations and reconciliation is a necessary basis for any growth and development.

  • Green Economy

    We see the transition to a low-carbon, green growth agenda as means of leading in the fight against climate change while creating profit, high-paying jobs, attracting investment, and boosting export opportunities. In fact, the global market for green economy goods and services is estimated to be worth more than $5 trillion with clean technology being one of the fastest growing sectors in the world. Our services include working with local clean technology export development and market studies with a focus on market intelligence, investment attraction, and trade. As well as strategic planning to help you make informed decisions about cleantech opportunities most suitable to your sector.
