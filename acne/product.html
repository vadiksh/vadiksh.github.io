  • What is Menstrual Acne?

    Menstrual acne is a subset of hormonal acne. Like the latter, menstrual acne also occurs because the hormones go a little haywire before our periods. Drops in our Estrogen / Progesterone levels leaves the male sex hormone, Androgens at a high in our bodies. This level of androgens can cause amok with our skin leaving it more oily and vulnerable to acne. These conditions make it very easy for period pimple to sneak in.
