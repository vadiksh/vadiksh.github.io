Questions & Answers

  • What is the format of your data file?
    Data is provided in CSV format for easy manipulation.
  • What are my data delivery choices?

    Delivery choices include API, Amazon AWS Cloud, AWS S3, FTP, disk, and Virtual Data Drive.

    Visit DATA DELIVERY page to see details

    Click DOWNLOAD VDD to download Virtual Data Drive

  • Do you include deleted symbols?
    All AlgoSeek equity datasets include symbols that have been delisted due to bankruptcy, M&A or any other reason.
  • What is timestamp?
    Each event is timestamped to the millisecond.
  • Do you have updates?
    Daily and weekly update subscriptions are available.
  • Can you do Custom fields and data?

    AlgoSeek has built flexible robust cloud infrastructure with large parallel processing capability. We can run 1,000+ computer instances concurrently for building custom datasets.

    AlgoSeek can build custom bars of any time length ( eg. One second or one hour). We can also build data sets with any fields calculated from our data or combined with client’s data.

    Please contact us about the custom data you require.

  • What do “as-is” and adjusted pricing mean?
    Stock raw data is provided “as-is” from the trading day to provide the most realistic backtesting and research. An adjustment factor file is available to compensate for corporate events like splits and dividents.
  • Do you filter data?
    The data is raw without any filtering to provide the most realistic dataset for research and backlisting.
  • What can I do with AlgoSeek data?
    AlgoSeek data is perfect for your institution’s historical research and backtesting.
  • Who are the AlgoSeek data users?
    Our data is used by professional traders, hedge funds, banks and universities.
  • Do you include extension hour trading?
    Data includes all pre and post market trading sessions.
