Questions & Answers

  • Do I need to provide payment information when I submit a new order?
    The order form is to help us understand your needs. Your payment information is not required for submitting.
  • How do I know the exact costs of datasets I have chosen?
    Our sales will contact you for pricing and contract signing.
  • Can I change my order after submission?
    Yes. You can discuss the changes you want to make with our sales rep.
  • Can I cancel my order after submission?
    Yes. You can find submitted orders under "My Orders" menu. And you can select the order and choose "cancel"
  • Can I rent data by filling the new order form?
    No. This Order Form is only for buy and lease option. Pleas visit data QuantGo if you want to rent data
  • Where can I find guidance on how to filling the Order Form?
    You can click the button beneath the title row to find guidance on how to fill the cell.
