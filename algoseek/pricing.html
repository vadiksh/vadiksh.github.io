Questions & Answers

  • How many users do I need to buy/lease?

    One license grants data access to one use case with no more than five users.

    For example, if you have two teams in your organization that need accesses to algoseek data for two separate projects, you’ll need to purchase (buy or lease) two licenses even if the total number of users is less than five; If the two teams collaborate on the same project and the total number of users does not exceed five, you need only one license; If you have a team of six people and all of them need access to algoseek data, you also need two licenses.

  • What rights do I have with a license?

    A institutional license grants access right to algoseek data to one use case with no more than five users. You may not redistribute algoseek data outside of your organization. You may not redistribute algoseek data internally to other use cases in the same organization. You have the freedom to distribute the data and/or analytics you derived from algoseek data if and only if the initial algoseek data cannot be derived from such data.

    A individual license grant access right to algoseek data to one individual.

  • Can I transfer my license to other users?

    A license grants data access to only one use case and you may not transfer the license to other use cases. But you may transfer the data access rights from one employee to another inside a use case in the event of a personnel change.

    If you are under an effective lease license, you can contact algoseek sales to register a license change.

  • Do I need to buy license for the data I derived from algoseek data?
    No. You have the freedom to distribute the data deriving from algoseek datasets if (and only if) such derived data will not lead to algoseek data.
  • Can I choose my license type?
    You don’t need to choose your license type. algoseek decides your license type according to your company type and size.
  • What SLA does algoseek grantee for the license I purchased/leased?
    Different license types have different default service level. Please contact algoseek sales for detail.
  • Can I upgrade to a higher SLA?
    Yes. If you want a service level higher than the default service level of your license, please contact algoseek sales to purchase an upgrade.
