3. Co-Lo Servers and Proprietary Ticker Plant

Data source matters.

Restrained by costs and lacking technological ability, market data vendors usually obtain data from exchange archives and/or third-party ticker plants.

Real-time data is imperfect, subject to exchange publishing mistakes such as out-of-sequence packets. Exchange archived data has been “corrected” and cannot be used to exactly replicate the live data. Using corrected data for backtesting risks algorithm lacking robustness and breaking in the real-time scenario.

When obtaining data from a third-party ticker plant, the data vendor has no control over what data points are recorded and how data is recorded. When data issues occur, the data vendor needs to ask the ticker plant owner to investigate.

With our NY4 co-lo servers and proprietary ticker plant, algoseek provides:

  • The best “as-is” data in the market
  • SLA & vertically integrated problem-solving capability
  • Custom ability without ticker plant limitation
